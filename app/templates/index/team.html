Meet the Officers

The Association for Computing Machinery University of Puget Sound Student Chapter presents its executive team members: Jesse, Thavy, Alex, and Patrick. The President oversees Chapter meeetings, conducts ACM annual report, and executes decisions on behalf of the ACM-UPS chapter. The Vice President oversees management of the website, entire executive team, and chapter members. The Treasurer oversees annual budget for the fiscal year. The Project Manager makes projects happen. This is the ACM-UPS Chapter executive team.
